Use the NetReconAgent allowlist and a fresh workspace.

Private/LAN target (needs opt‑in):
LSG_ALLOW_PRIVATE=1 ./shannon.mjs generate http://192.168.1.130:3000 -o ./shannon-results-nmap-$(date +%s) --agents NetReconAgent,OpenAPIDiscoveryAgent,SitemapAgent,NetReconAgen,CrawlerAgent,BrowserCrawlerAgent,TechFingerprinterAgent --no-resume --debug-tools -v

Public/domain target:
./shannon.mjs generate https://example.com -o ./shannon-results-nmap-$(date +%s) --agents NetReconAgent --no-resume --debug-tools -v
Notes

--agents NetReconAgent runs only nmap.
--no-resume forces execution even if prior results exist.
--debug-tools writes nmap logs to <workspace>/tool-logs (optional).
-v shows per‑agent completion in the console.
